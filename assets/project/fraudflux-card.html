A false alarm costs a phone call. A missed fraud costs someone's account.یک هشدار اشتباه، فقط یک تماس تلفنی هزینه دارد؛ یک تقلب ازدست‌رفته، حسابِ یک نفر را.

An end-to-end fraud detection pipeline trained on 284,807 real credit card transactions (0.172% fraud). Four candidate models were compared with per-model threshold sweeps — and the headline result is a change of mind: pure recall-first would have shipped a model with 629 false alarms; the deployed Random Forest at threshold 0.15 balances 81% recall with 80% precision and only 19 false alarms across 56,746 test transactions. Leakage-safe preprocessing (dedupe before split, stratified sampling, selective scaling) and an F2-scored GridSearchCV round out the pipeline.
